Elyx Mobility e-Bike

Description

The Elyx Mobility e-Bike is a versatile and efficient electric bicycle designed to enhance your daily commute and leisure rides. It offers a perfect blend of performance, comfort, and style, making it an excellent choice for riders of all levels.

Key Features

Feature Description
Battery High-capacity lithium-ion battery with a range of up to 50 miles on a single charge.
Motor Efficient 350W electric motor for smooth acceleration and a top speed of 20 mph.
Frame Durable and lightweight aluminum frame with ergonomic design for comfortable riding.
Brakes Front and rear disc brakes for precise and reliable stopping power.
Tires 28-inch puncture-resistant tires for a stable and smooth ride on various terrains.
