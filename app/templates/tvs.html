TVS iQube Electric e-Bike

Description

The TVS iQube Electric e-Bike is a smart and efficient electric scooter designed for urban mobility. It combines advanced technology, eco-friendliness, and convenience to offer a futuristic and eco-conscious mode of transportation.

Key Features

Feature Description
Battery High-capacity lithium-ion battery with a range of up to 75 miles on a single charge.
Motor Quiet and efficient electric motor for smooth acceleration and a top speed of 40 mph.
Design Sleek and modern design with premium build quality and a comfortable riding position.
Connectivity Integrated smartphone app for navigation, vehicle diagnostics, and smart features.
Safety Advanced safety features, including ABS, regenerative braking, and LED lighting.
