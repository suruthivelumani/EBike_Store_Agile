Turnerex e-Bike

Description

The Turnerex e-Bike is an innovative and versatile electric bicycle designed for urban commuting and outdoor adventures. With its cutting-edge features and stylish design, it's the perfect choice for riders of all levels.

Key Features

Feature Description
Battery Advanced lithium-polymer battery with a range of up to 70 miles on a single charge.
Motor Efficient 350W electric motor for a smooth and silent ride with adjustable power modes.
Frame Durable and lightweight aluminum frame with a sleek and modern design.
Suspension Front fork suspension and rear shock absorber for comfortable off-road riding.
Tires 29-inch multi-terrain tires for excellent traction on various surfaces.
