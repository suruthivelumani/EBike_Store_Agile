Ola S1 Pro Electric Scooter

Description

The Ola S1 Pro is a premium electric scooter that offers an eco-friendly and convenient way to travel in the city. With its advanced features and sleek design, it's the perfect choice for urban commuters.

Key Features

Feature Description
Battery High-capacity lithium-ion battery with a range of up to 85 miles on a single charge.
Motor Powerful 4.2 kW electric motor for quick acceleration and a top speed of 60 mph.
Design Sleek and modern design with premium materials and customizable color options.
Display Interactive touchscreen display for navigation, music, and vehicle settings.
Safety Advanced safety features, including ABS, regenerative braking, and LED lighting.
