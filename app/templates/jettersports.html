Jetter Sports e-Bike

Description

The Jetter Sports e-Bike is a high-performance electric bicycle designed for sports enthusiasts. Whether you're tackling tough trails or speeding through the city, this e-Bike offers an exhilarating ride.

Key Features

Feature Description
Battery Advanced lithium-ion battery with a range of up to 60 miles on a single charge.
Motor High-powered 500W electric motor for rapid acceleration and hill climbing.
Frame Lightweight aluminum alloy frame with sporty geometry for agile handling.
Suspension Front and rear suspension for a smooth and comfortable ride over rough terrain.
Tires 27.5-inch all-terrain tires with excellent grip and durability.
