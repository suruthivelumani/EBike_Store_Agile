Zooba eBike

Description

The Zooba eBike is a stylish and eco-friendly electric bicycle designed for urban commuting and leisurely rides. With its modern features and ergonomic design, it offers a convenient and enjoyable way to travel.

Key Features

Feature Description
Battery Long-lasting lithium-ion battery with a range of up to 40 miles on a single charge.
Motor Efficient 250W electric motor for smooth and quiet riding.
Frame Lightweight and durable aluminum frame with a comfortable riding position.
Brakes Front and rear disc brakes for reliable stopping power in various conditions.
Tires 28-inch puncture-resistant tires for a comfortable and secure ride.
