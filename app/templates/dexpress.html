Dexpress e-Bike

Description

The Dexpress e-Bike is a cutting-edge electric bicycle designed for both urban commuting and off-road adventures. It combines innovative technology with a sleek and ergonomic design, making it an ideal choice for riders of all levels.

Key Features

Feature Description
Battery Powerful lithium-ion battery with a range of up to 60 miles on a single charge.
Motor High-torque 500W electric motor for quick acceleration and versatile riding.
Frame Lightweight and durable aluminum frame with adjustable geometry for comfort.
Suspension Front suspension fork and rear shock absorber for a smooth ride on various terrains.
Tires 27.5-inch all-terrain tires with excellent traction and puncture resistance.
