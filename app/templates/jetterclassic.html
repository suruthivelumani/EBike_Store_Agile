Jetter Classic e-Bike

Description

The Jetter Classic e-Bike is a stylish and eco-friendly electric bicycle that combines modern technology with classic design. It's perfect for both urban commuting and leisurely rides in the countryside.

Key Features

Feature Description
Battery Long-lasting lithium-ion battery with a range of up to 50 miles on a single charge.
Motor Powerful 250W electric motor for smooth and efficient riding.
Frame Classic steel frame with a comfortable upright riding position.
Brakes Front and rear disc brakes for reliable stopping power.
Tires 26-inch puncture-resistant tires for a comfortable and secure ride.
